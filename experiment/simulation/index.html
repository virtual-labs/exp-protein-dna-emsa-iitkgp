Instructions
  1. Click on " Click to Start the Experiment" button.
  2. Next click on checkboxes of step 1 and click on "Annealing fluorescently-labelled DNA probe" button.
  3. Next click on all the checkbox in step 2 and click on "Prepare Protien Sample" button.
  4. Now, select correct components from the dropdown for mixing of protein sample.
  5. Click on "Incubate in ice bucket" button.
  6. Next click on "Run the gel in electrophoresis buffer" button.
  7. Now, connect the negative wire of electrophorersis chamber to negative of voltage meter and positive wire of electrophorersis chamber to positive wire of voltage meter.
  8. Now click on "V" and "+" sign to set the voltage between 100 V and 120 V.
  9. Click on the run icon button to run the gel.The dye front will reaches the bottom of the gel in dark conditions
  10. Click on "Visualize the protein-DNA" under UV light
